Mushroom Risotto

This is Mushroom Risotto, not to be confused with "Mushroom Risotto."

Ah yes, mushroom risotto. An old classic of Italy (I think). Mushroom Risotto is a dish known to have originated from the great man known as Otto, known for having exceptional "rizz." Thus the name risotto! This is a dish that is delicious and eaasy to master, so long as you have two working arms. Let's get started with how to make it!

Ingredients:

  • Portobello Mushrooms
  • Arbolio Rice
  • Onions/Shallots
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Peppah
  • Oil
